Mission

The Culinary Theory is here to make food preparation enjoyable and stress-free. We provide culinary tips and recipes written just for home cooks. We're all for assisting in "kitchen wins," after all.

Diversity and Inclusion

The Culinary Theory is open to everyone, regardless of their color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, background, or abilities. Every aspect of the website, from the people we hire to the recipes we offer, reflects our purposeful effort to be a resource for every home cook.
